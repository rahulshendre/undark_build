# RBI communication rules for collections

This file isolates the communication constraints because they directly shape
what Undark can recommend.

## Rule: Lawful hours only
- **Why it exists:** To prevent harassment through untimely contact.
- **Operational implication:** Contact the borrower only within permitted hours. TODO: verify exact window (commonly cited as 08:00–19:00) against current source.
- **Evidence needed:** Time-stamped contact logs.
- **Penalty if violated:** Complaint grounds; regulatory action.

## Rule: Borrower-only (no third-party disclosure)
- **Why it exists:** Privacy and dignity.
- **Operational implication:** Communicate with the borrower (and guarantor where applicable), not relatives/neighbours/employers, except narrow location-finding without revealing the debt.
- **Evidence needed:** Records showing contact stayed with the right party.
- **Penalty if violated:** Privacy/conduct violation.

## Rule: Accurate, non-threatening content
- **Why it exists:** Communications must not deceive or intimidate.
- **Operational implication:** State the correct amount and the correct legal basis. Do not threaten consequences that do not legally apply (e.g. arrest for a civil debt, or NI Act action where no cheque exists).
- **Evidence needed:** Copies of every message/notice.
- **Penalty if violated:** Misrepresentation undermines the case and invites action.

## Rule: Reasonable frequency; respect opt-outs and sensitive events
- **Why it exists:** Repeated or ill-timed contact is harassment.
- **Operational implication:** Keep contact frequency reasonable; pause on bereavement or genuine hardship; honour legitimate requests. TODO: verify any explicit frequency caps.
- **Evidence needed:** Contact-frequency logs.
- **Penalty if violated:** Harassment finding; regulatory action.

## Rule: Written intimation before escalation
- **Why it exists:** Fair warning and an evidence trail before formal/legal steps.
- **Operational implication:** Send a written demand/notice before escalating; keep proof of dispatch and delivery. This is also what later forums expect to see.
- **Evidence needed:** Notice copy + proof of dispatch (courier/registered post/email) and, ideally, delivery.
- **Penalty if violated:** Weaker position at Lok Adalat/court; possible conduct issue.

## Operational takeaway for Undark
Every recommended communication must: target the borrower only, within lawful
hours, state a reconciled amount, assert only a legal basis that actually
applies, and be preserved as evidence. These map directly to compliance_flags.
