# MFIN Micrometer Synopsis — 48th issue (data as on 31 Dec 2023)

**Document purpose:** Industry overview of the microfinance (micro-credit) sector
— portfolio outstanding and outreach across lender types, plus portfolio-at-risk
trends. Section I uses CRIF High Mark data; Section II uses self-reported data
from 48 of 51 MFIN-member NBFC-MFIs.

## Key operational takeaways
- The microfinance universe spans NBFC-MFIs, Banks, Small Finance Banks, and
  NBFCs — the core small-ticket, largely-unsecured segment Undark serves.
- Strong YoY portfolio growth across all lender types as on Dec-2023.
