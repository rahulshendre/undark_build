# National Lok Adalat — 14.03.2026 (All Types of Cases)

**Document purpose:** Official NALSA tabulation of cases taken up, disposed, and
settlement amounts in the National Lok Adalat of 14 March 2026, state-wise, split
into pre-litigation and pending cases plus a total.

## Key operational takeaways
- Lok Adalats clear cases at very large national scale in a single day — context
  for why Lok Adalat is the realistic small-ticket settlement channel.
- The table separates **pre-litigation** from **pending** cases, the two ways a
  recovery matter reaches Lok Adalat.

## Verified figures (Grand Total)
Columns confirmed by arithmetic (pre-litigation + pending = total):

| Category | Cases taken up | Cases disposed | Settlement amount (Rs) |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Pre-litigation | 42,993,421 | 34,687,917 | 78,214,367,039 |
| Pending | 8,324,259 | 5,951,139 | 159,577,692,560 |
| **Total** | **51,317,680** | **40,639,056** | **237,792,059,599** |

Total settlement amount ≈ Rs 23,779.21 crore.

## Important notes (from the report itself)
- Delhi organized its National Lok Adalat on 22-03-2026 (not fully in this date's totals).
- West Bengal postponed its National Lok Adalat (not reflected).
