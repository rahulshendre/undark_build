# NBFC Scale Based Regulation Master Direction, 2023 (RBI/DoR/2023-24/106)

**Document purpose:** Consolidates RBI's NBFC regulations under the Scale Based
Regulation framework. Includes the **Fair Practices Code** provisions that
govern NBFC conduct, including recovery and the engagement of recovery agents.
