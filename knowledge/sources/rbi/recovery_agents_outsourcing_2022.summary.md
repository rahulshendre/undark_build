# Outsourcing of Financial Services — Recovery Agents (RBI/2022-23/108)

**Document purpose:** Establishes mandatory conduct standards for recovery
agents employed by regulated entities (REs) to prevent intimidation and
harassment during debt collection.

## Applies to
All Commercial Banks (excluding Payments Banks); All-India Financial Institutions
(Exim Bank, NABARD, NHB, SIDBI, NaBFID); all NBFCs including Housing Finance
Companies; Primary (Urban)/State/District Central Co-operative Banks; all Asset
Reconstruction Companies.

## Key operational takeaways
- The RE is accountable for its recovery agents' conduct — outsourcing does not outsource responsibility.
- A hard time window governs contact: **no calls before 8:00 a.m. or after 7:00 p.m.** for recovery of overdue loans.
- Harassment in any form is prohibited and defined broadly.

## Important rules (as stated on the page)
- **Para 2 — No intimidation or harassment:** REs shall ensure they or their
  agents do not resort to intimidation or harassment of any kind, verbal or
  physical, including acts intended to humiliate publicly or intrude upon the
  privacy of the debtors' family members, sending inappropriate messages,
  making threatening and/or anonymous calls, or persistently calling the
  borrower.
- **Para 2 — Contact hours:** prohibits "calling the borrower before 8:00 a.m.
  and after 7:00 p.m. for recovery of overdue loans."

## Sections relevant to loan recovery
- Para 2 (conduct of recovery agents; contact-hour restriction) — directly
  governs what a practitioner may and may not do when recovering, and what a
  borrower can complain about.
