# Borrower Communication (template snippets)

> Short messages for routine contact. Keep factual and non-coercive. Apply the
> compliance rails in `rbi/communication-rules.md`.

## Payment reminder (SMS/WhatsApp)
```
Dear [BORROWER_NAME], an amount of Rs. [OVERDUE_AMOUNT] on your loan a/c
[LOAN_ACCOUNT_NO] with [LENDER_NAME] is overdue as on [AS_ON_DATE]. Please pay
to keep your account regular. Already paid? Share the reference at [CONTACT].
- [LENDER_NAME]
```

## Settlement opportunity (SMS/WhatsApp)
```
Dear [BORROWER_NAME], [LENDER_NAME] is open to settling your loan a/c
[LOAN_ACCOUNT_NO]. To discuss options, please contact [CONTACT] by
[RESPONSE_BY_DATE]. - [LENDER_NAME]
```

## Acknowledgement of payment
```
Dear [BORROWER_NAME], we have received Rs. [AMOUNT_RECEIVED] towards loan a/c
[LOAN_ACCOUNT_NO] on [PAYMENT_DATE]. Updated outstanding: Rs.
[UPDATED_OUTSTANDING]. Thank you. - [LENDER_NAME]
```

## Email — request to discuss
```
Subject: Regarding your loan account [LOAN_ACCOUNT_NO]

Dear [BORROWER_NAME],

We would like to discuss the status of your loan account [LOAN_ACCOUNT_NO] with
[LENDER_NAME] and the options available to you, including settlement. Please
reply to this email or call [CONTACT] at a convenient time.

Regards,
[SENDER_NAME], for [LENDER_NAME]
```

## Compliance rails (every message)
- Borrower-only — never message relatives/neighbours/employers about the debt.
- Lawful hours only.
- State an accurate amount; assert only legal bases that apply.
- No threats, no pressure, no public disclosure.
- Retain message logs as evidence.
