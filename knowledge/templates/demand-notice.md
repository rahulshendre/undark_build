# Demand Notice (template)

> Placeholders in `[BRACKETS]`. Never hardcode borrower details. The amount must
> be reconciled to the documents; the legal basis must match the actual facts.

```
[DATE]

To,
[BORROWER_NAME]
[BORROWER_ADDRESS]

Subject: Demand for payment of outstanding dues — Loan Account No. [LOAN_ACCOUNT_NO]

Dear [BORROWER_NAME],

This is with reference to the loan availed by you from [LENDER_NAME] under Loan
Account No. [LOAN_ACCOUNT_NO] dated [LOAN_DATE].

As per our records, a sum of Rs. [OUTSTANDING_AMOUNT] (Rupees
[OUTSTANDING_AMOUNT_WORDS]) is outstanding and payable as on [AS_ON_DATE],
details of which are set out in the enclosed statement of account.

You are hereby called upon to pay the said outstanding amount of Rs.
[OUTSTANDING_AMOUNT] within [NOTICE_PERIOD_DAYS] days of receipt of this notice.

Should you wish to discuss repayment or a settlement, please contact the
undersigned at [CONTACT].

This notice is issued in the ordinary course of recovery and without prejudice
to [LENDER_NAME]'s rights and remedies available under law.

Yours faithfully,
[SENDER_NAME]
[SENDER_DESIGNATION]
For [LENDER_NAME]
[CONTACT]

Enclosure: Statement of account as on [AS_ON_DATE]
```

## Compliance checklist (before sending)
- [ ] Amount reconciles to the statement of account.
- [ ] Legal basis stated is accurate; **no threats of consequences that do not legally apply**.
- [ ] Addressed to the borrower (and guarantor if applicable) only.
- [ ] Sent by a trackable mode; **retain proof of dispatch**.
- [ ] Reviewed by a human before sending. Undark never sends.
