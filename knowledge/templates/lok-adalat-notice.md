# Lok Adalat Notice (template)

> Invites the borrower to resolve through a Lok Adalat reference under the Legal
> Services Authorities Act, 1987. Consent-based and final once an award is made.

```
[DATE]

To,
[BORROWER_NAME]
[BORROWER_ADDRESS]

Subject: Opportunity to settle outstanding dues through Lok Adalat —
Loan Account No. [LOAN_ACCOUNT_NO]

Dear [BORROWER_NAME],

The outstanding amount of Rs. [OUTSTANDING_AMOUNT] (Rupees
[OUTSTANDING_AMOUNT_WORDS]) on Loan Account No. [LOAN_ACCOUNT_NO] remains unpaid
as on [AS_ON_DATE].

[LENDER_NAME] proposes to resolve this matter amicably through the Lok Adalat
mechanism under the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987. A settlement reached in
Lok Adalat is recorded as an award having the status of a civil court decree and
is final and binding on both parties.

You are invited to participate and settle the matter. Please contact the
undersigned at [CONTACT] on or before [RESPONSE_BY_DATE] to indicate your
willingness and discuss the proposed settlement terms / sitting at
[LOK_ADALAT_VENUE_OR_DATE_IF_KNOWN].

This is a without-prejudice settlement opportunity.

Yours faithfully,
[SENDER_NAME]
[SENDER_DESIGNATION]
For [LENDER_NAME]
[CONTACT]
```

## Compliance checklist (before sending)
- [ ] Amount reconciled to documents.
- [ ] Framed as an **invitation to settle** (consent-based), not a threat.
- [ ] Settlement figure is realistic and authorized.
- [ ] Borrower-only; trackable dispatch; proof retained.
- [ ] Human-reviewed.
