# Pre-litigation Notice (template)

> The last notice before formal action. Builds the good-faith evidence trail.
> State only a legal basis that actually applies to the case.

```
[DATE]

To,
[BORROWER_NAME]
[BORROWER_ADDRESS]

Subject: Final notice before initiation of recovery proceedings —
Loan Account No. [LOAN_ACCOUNT_NO]

Dear [BORROWER_NAME],

Despite earlier communication(s) dated [PRIOR_NOTICE_DATES], the outstanding
amount of Rs. [OUTSTANDING_AMOUNT] (Rupees [OUTSTANDING_AMOUNT_WORDS]) on Loan
Account No. [LOAN_ACCOUNT_NO] remains unpaid as on [AS_ON_DATE].

You are called upon to pay the said amount within [NOTICE_PERIOD_DAYS] days of
receipt of this notice, failing which [LENDER_NAME] may initiate appropriate
recovery proceedings, including reference to [FORUM e.g. Lok Adalat] and/or
[OTHER_APPLICABLE_REMEDY], in accordance with law, entirely at your risk as to
cost and consequences.

We remain open to an amicable resolution. To discuss settlement, contact the
undersigned at [CONTACT].

This notice is without prejudice to all rights and remedies of [LENDER_NAME].

Yours faithfully,
[SENDER_NAME]
[SENDER_DESIGNATION]
For [LENDER_NAME]
[CONTACT]

Enclosure: Statement of account as on [AS_ON_DATE]
```

## Compliance checklist (before sending)
- [ ] Every prior notice referenced actually exists with proof.
- [ ] The forum/remedy named **actually applies** (e.g. do not threaten NI Act §138 if no cheque bounced; do not threaten SARFAESI on an unsecured loan).
- [ ] Amount reconciled.
- [ ] Borrower-only; trackable dispatch; proof retained.
- [ ] Human-reviewed. Undark never sends.
