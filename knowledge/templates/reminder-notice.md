# Reminder Notice (template)

> A softer, earlier-stage reminder. Same compliance rails as any communication.

```
[DATE]

To,
[BORROWER_NAME]
[BORROWER_ADDRESS]

Subject: Reminder — overdue instalment(s) on Loan Account No. [LOAN_ACCOUNT_NO]

Dear [BORROWER_NAME],

We wish to remind you that as per our records, an amount of Rs. [OVERDUE_AMOUNT]
towards Loan Account No. [LOAN_ACCOUNT_NO] is currently overdue as on
[AS_ON_DATE].

We request you to clear the overdue amount at the earliest to keep your account
regular and avoid further charges as per your loan terms.

If you have already made the payment, kindly ignore this reminder and share the
payment reference at [CONTACT]. If you are facing difficulty, please reach us to
discuss the options available to you.

Regards,
[SENDER_NAME]
[SENDER_DESIGNATION]
For [LENDER_NAME]
[CONTACT]
```

## Compliance checklist (before sending)
- [ ] Overdue amount is accurate.
- [ ] Tone is factual; no threat or pressure.
- [ ] Borrower-only; lawful contact mode.
- [ ] Retain a copy and proof of dispatch.
- [ ] Human-reviewed.
