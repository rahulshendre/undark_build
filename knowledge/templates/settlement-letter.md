# Settlement Letter (template)

> Records the agreed settlement (often a One-Time Settlement). Once accepted and
> paid per terms, it closes the account. Terms must be authorized.

```
[DATE]

To,
[BORROWER_NAME]
[BORROWER_ADDRESS]

Subject: Settlement of Loan Account No. [LOAN_ACCOUNT_NO]

Dear [BORROWER_NAME],

With reference to your loan account No. [LOAN_ACCOUNT_NO] with [LENDER_NAME],
having an outstanding of Rs. [OUTSTANDING_AMOUNT] as on [AS_ON_DATE],
[LENDER_NAME] is agreeable to settle the account on the following terms:

1. Settlement amount: Rs. [SETTLEMENT_AMOUNT] (Rupees [SETTLEMENT_AMOUNT_WORDS]).
2. Payment schedule: [PAYMENT_SCHEDULE — e.g. lump sum by [PAY_BY_DATE], or
   instalments of Rs. [X] on [DATES]].
3. Mode of payment: [PAYMENT_MODE — to [LENDER_NAME]'s account as specified].
4. On receipt of the full settlement amount as above, the account shall be
   treated as settled and closed, and [LENDER_NAME] shall issue a closure /
   no-dues confirmation.

This offer is valid until [OFFER_VALID_UNTIL]. Non-payment as per the schedule
will render this settlement void, and the original outstanding shall stand
revived.

Credit-bureau status will be reported in accordance with applicable rules.
[TODO: confirm exact reporting wording per current rules.]

Yours faithfully,
[SENDER_NAME]
[SENDER_DESIGNATION]
For [LENDER_NAME]
[CONTACT]

Accepted by borrower:
__________________________   Date: __________
[BORROWER_NAME]
```

- [ ] Settlement amount and schedule are authorized.
- [ ] Revival-on-default and validity clauses included.
- [ ] Closure/no-dues commitment stated.
